Privacy Policy

Rune Triad: Hidden World — Last updated July 2026

This policy explains what information Rune Triad: Hidden World ("the App") collects and how it's used.

What we collect

  • Local progress data — your coin balance, relic progress, and settings (such as sound preferences) are stored on your device. This data is not sent to us.
  • Leaderboard name and score — if you choose to submit a score to the leaderboard, your chosen display name and best relic reached are stored and shown to other players of the App. Do not use your real name or any personal information as your leaderboard name.

What we don't do

  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any device permissions.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only optional data field is a self-chosen leaderboard nickname, which should never contain real names or identifying details.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact details listed on the app's store listing page.
